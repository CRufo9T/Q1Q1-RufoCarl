Porco Rosso

Porco Rosso is a animated film from studio Ghibli created in 1992. The story is about an italian WW1 ex-fighter ace. Since the war is over, he then starts bounty hunting chasing pirates in the Adriatic Sea. But a curse has turned him into a pig. He was once called Marco Pagot, but he is now know as "Porco Rosso" which mean Crimson Pig.
